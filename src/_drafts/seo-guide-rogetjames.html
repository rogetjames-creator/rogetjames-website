How to build extraordinary Google reach for rogetjames.com

First

The Honest Reality

No one can guarantee a top Google ranking — anyone who promises that is selling you something. What IS true is that for a niche like laser-cut wall art in Australia, the competition is thin. Doing the right things well and consistently will get you there.

Google asks three questions about every website:

01

Is it relevant?

Does the page use the words people actually search for?

02

Is it authoritative?

Do other sites link to it and talk about it?

03

Is it good?

Do people stay on it or leave immediately?

04

Is it fast & accessible?

Does it load quickly and work on phones?

The Tactics

How to Get Great Google Pickups

🟠 Google Business Profile — Do This First

Free. Takes 20 minutes. This is what puts you on Google Maps and in the panel that appears on the right side of search results when someone searches "ROGETjames" or "laser cut wall art Perth".

Go to: business.google.com

Add your business name, category (Artist / Art Gallery / Metal Fabricator), all three locations, phone, website, hours if applicable, and photos of your work. The more complete the profile, the higher Google ranks it.

Add at least 10–15 photos of your work. Google Business images appear directly in search results.

🟠 Get Other Sites to Link to Yours

This is the single biggest ranking factor after content. Each link from another website is effectively a vote of confidence. Quality beats quantity — one link from an architecture firm's website is worth more than 50 directory listings.

  • Architects & designers you've worked with — ask them to credit ROGETjames and link to your site on their project pages
  • Hederablu, Entanglements, WG Outdoor Life — your agents should all have a live link to rogetjames.com
  • Houzz — free profile, high domain authority, links back to your site. Create a profile at houzz.com.au and upload your best project photos
  • ArchiPro — Australian architecture & design directory, very relevant audience
  • True Local / Yellow Pages — free listings, easy backlinks
  • Any past press coverage — chase any editorial or blog that mentioned your work and ask for a live link if there isn't one already

🟠 Pinterest — Underrated for Art

Pinterest is a visual search engine in its own right. Laser cut and metal art performs extremely well there. Every pin links back to your site, and Google reads Pinterest as a signal of content quality. Create a ROGETjames Pinterest account, pin your best work, and link every pin to rogetjames.com. Takes an hour to set up and keeps working for years.

🟠 Instagram Profile Link

Make sure your Instagram bio links to rogetjames.com — not the Netlify preview URL. When the domain is live, update it. Instagram links are "nofollow" but the traffic signal still matters to Google.

🟠 Google Search Console — After Launch

Free Google tool. Submit your sitemap here the day the domain goes live. Google will crawl your site within days rather than weeks. Also shows you what search terms people are already finding you with — gold for knowing what's working.

Go to: search.google.com/search-console

Add rogetjames.com as a property, verify ownership (Netlify makes this easy — paste a DNS record), then submit: https://rogetjames.com/sitemap.xml

Site Improvements

What Can Be Built Into the Site

FAQ Schema

Google shows FAQ answers as expanded results in search — taking up 3–4× more space on the page than a regular result. I can add an invisible FAQ block to the site targeting questions like:

  • "How long does laser cut wall art take to deliver?"
  • "Do you deliver laser cut art Australia wide?"
  • "Can I get custom sized laser cut wall art?"
  • "What is Corten steel and how does it weather?"
  • "Do you do bespoke commissions?"

Richer Crawlable Text

Google cannot read images. The site is visually stunning but currently light on text that Google can crawl. Adding short, keyword-rich descriptions to each design series — visible to Google, not cluttering the design — significantly improves relevance signals.

Bespoke Commissions Image Alt Text

The Bespoke Commissions section has hundreds of project images that currently have empty alt text. Each one is a missed opportunity to tell Google what's in the image. Adding descriptions like "ZARATHSTRA corten steel gate commission — Cottesloe Hotel Perth" builds relevance over time.
